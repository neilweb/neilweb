My Approach to Coding

The approach I take to coding changes with increased knowledge and technical growth. But first step is to visualize and draw the wireframes of the envisioned website.

When you position the wireframes, decide whether to use grid, flex or both. Keep in mind that flexbox works great along with grid and the website tends to be more responsive.

I can help you with some flexboxes and grids.
